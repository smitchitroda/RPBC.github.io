Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com.)

Morning Shift - Girls Only (સવારની બેચ - બહેનો માટે)
Afternoon Shift - Boys Only (બપોરની બેચ - ભાઈઓ માટે)

The Bachelor of Commerce degree provides wide range of managerial skills, while building competence in a particular area of business. Students are exposed to general business principles, taking courses in accounting, finance, income tax, investment banking, etc.

Dureaiton : 6 Semesters [03 Years]


Eligibility : Higher Secondary Exam (10 + 2) passed in any discipline from Gujarat Higher Education Board OR Higher Secondary Exam (10 + 2) passed in any discipline from other board recognized by Saurashtra University.


Medium : English


Syllabus : (As per Saurashtra University, Rajkot)
