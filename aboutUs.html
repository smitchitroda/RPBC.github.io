Shree R. P. Bhalodia college of commerce,management and computer was established in the year of 1998 by Shree R. P. Patel Trust Mumbai. Their mission is the enrichment of students and prepared them to be the successful future entrepreneurs and techorats by pouring educationwith resources from intellectual academic professional.

The College of Commerce, Management & Computer offers courses in the areas of Information Technology, Management at Bachelor levels like Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com.), Bachelor of Business Administration (B.B.A.), Bachelor of Computer Applications (B.C.A.)
