Welcome to RPBC - Rajkot

Shree R. P. Bhalodia college of commerce, management and computer was established in the year of 1998 by Shree R. P. Patel Trust Mumbai. Their mission is the enrichment of students and prepared them to be the successful future entrepreneurs and technocrats by pouring education with resources from intellectual academic professionals.

Qualified Faculties

College possesses young, energetic, dynamic and qualified as per University norms. They inspire, mentor, motivate their students to achieve their dreams.

Library

College has rich library with various books / magazines of Management, Commerce, Computer, Literature and General Awareness too.

Class Rooms

College has well equipped class rooms with advanced resources. The Teaching-Learning pedagogy used in the classrooms is IT enabled and all classrooms.

Computer Labs

The college has high-tech six computer labs with twenty computers in each with well equipped latest softwares and internet facility.

Courses we offer

The College of Commerce, Management & Computer offers courses in the areas of Information Technology, Management at Bachelor levels like
Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com.), Bachelor of Business Administration (B.B.A.), Bachelor of Computer Applications (B.C.A.)

Bachelor of Business Administration (B.B.A.)

Bachelor of Business Administration degree is professional undergraduate course in Business Management and it's a one of the most popular bachelor degree programs. Help to develop the student's practical, managerial and communication skills, and business decision-making capability.
